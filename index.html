"La confluence"
Informations

Confluence is the new southern sector of the 2nd arrondissement of Lyon, located at the southern end of the peninsula of Lyon, near the confluence of the Rhone and Saone rivers. Coming from a ZAC since the late 1990s, the district is destined to profound changes.

Superficie 150 ha
Urbanisation step XXIe
Arrondissement Lyon 2e
Région Rhône-alpes

"Ile Barbe"
Informations

Isle Barbe is an island located in the middle of the Saone, in the 9th district of Lyon, in the district of Saint-Rambert-l'Ile-Barbe (former municipality annexed in 1963). Its name comes from the Latin insula barbara, "the wild island", testifying to what it was only late occupied (two centuries after the edges of Saone at the foot of the hill of Fourvière).

Culminate point 171 m
Geology Fluvial isle
Arrondissement Lyon 9e
Région Rhône-alpes
